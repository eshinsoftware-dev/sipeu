Loan Application Process

Step 1: Application Submission

  • Member fills out loan application form
  • Submit to Loans Officer at front counter
  • Provincial members submit via fax

Step 2: Eligibility Check

  • Loans Officer verifies eligibility
  • 70% recovery rule applied
  • Application filed and sent to Credit Committee

Step 3: Approval Process

  • Credit Committee reviews applications
  • Ensure compliance with lending policy
  • Approved applications signed by 3 members
  • Rejected applications returned

Step 4: Payment Processing

  • Accountant verifies documents
  • Payment via check or bank transfer
  • Records updated in system

Step 5: Reconciliation & Reporting

  • Monthly reconciliation of accounts
  • Loan & share balance verification
  • Reports submitted to management and board
