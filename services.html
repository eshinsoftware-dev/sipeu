Loan Application Process

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the basic criteria as a public servant and review our schemes.

Step 2: Submit Document

Provide your recent payslip, valid ID, and completed application form.

Step 3: Assessment

Our team reviews your application swiftly and fairly.

Step 4: Disbursement

Upon approval, funds are transferred directly to your designated account.

Advance Scheme

Our Advance Scheme allows you to access a portion of your upcoming salary. A flat 5% interest rate applies to the advanced amount.

Flat Interest Rate: 5%

Salary Range Maximum Advance Eligibility
$1,000 - $3,000 Up to 30%
$3,001 - $5,000 Up to 40%
Above $5,000 Up to 50%
